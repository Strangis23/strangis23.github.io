How to Play

Goal

Clear 100 waves by building defenses with Tetris pieces. Protect your home base — the first piece you place.

You lose if base HP hits zero, the base is line-cleared, or blocks reach the spawn zone (top). Enemies on your base deal siege damage over time.

Phases

  • Place base — Drop your first piece anywhere; it becomes your home base.
  • Build — Place 4 pieces per round from your deck (20 cards, shuffled).
  • Wave — Enemies path toward the base; placed cells attack by card role.
  • Shop — Every 5 waves, buy a card and remove one from your deck to swap.

Controls

  • ← → move · ↓ soft drop · Space hard drop
  • ↑ / R rotate CW · Z rotate CCW · C hold
  • F cycle wave speed (during waves) · P pause
  • Tap Pad in the HUD to show on-screen buttons. Swipe the board to move; tap to rotate; tap damaged cells to repair.

Points & shop

Earn points from enemy kills, line clears, and wall passive income each wave. Spend points on card swaps or Fortify Base (+30 max base HP per purchase, escalating cost).

The shop can offer higher-tier walls: Bunker (rare) and Fortress (epic), in addition to Brick, Reinforced Wall, and Citadel.

Reading the board

Each placed cell shows its role color, a pattern overlay, and a glyph. Your home base keeps its role look but adds a gold border and ★ in the corner.

  • ▧ Wall — diagonal hatch · high HP, passive income, blocks walkers
  • • Shooter — target ring · basic single-target
  • ⊙ Sniper — crosshair · long range, high damage
  • ✺ Splash — burst lines · AoE shells
  • ❄ Slow — ripple rings · slows nearby enemies
  • ⁂ Gunner — four-way ticks · fast fire, all directions
  • ➤ Piercer — arrow · shots through multiple enemies
  • ✹ Multishot — spread · fan of projectiles

Rarity shows as a colored outline. Synergy (same role adjacent) adds a gold outline.

Enemy counters

Tower roles deal bonus damage to some enemies and reduced damage to others (1.5× weak, 0.55× resist):

  • Walker — weak: gunner, shooter · resist: splash, slow
  • Flyer — weak: sniper, piercer · resist: shooter, splash
  • Brute — weak: piercer, splash · resist: sniper, gunner
  • Boss — weak: multishot, slow · resist: shooter, gunner

Tower roles

  • Wall — High HP, passive income, no attack
  • Shooter — Basic single-target
  • Sniper — Long range, high damage
  • Splash — AoE shells
  • Slow — Slows nearby enemies
  • Gunner — Fast fire, all directions
  • Piercer — Fast shots through multiple enemies
  • Multishot — Spread fire

Synergy

Adjacent blocks of the same role boost HP and attack power. Synergized cells show a gold outline — no pop-up text.

Line clears & repair

Full rows clear for bonus points but destroy towers in those rows (cards stay in your deck).

During build, click a damaged cell to fully repair it:

  • Towers — 10 pts per missing HP, multiplied by rarity (common ×1, uncommon ×1.3, rare ×1.6, epic ×2.2, legendary ×3)
  • Home base — repairs the shared base HP pool at 4× the tower rate (40 pts per missing HP)

Tips

  • Pick a defensible spot for your first piece.
  • Prune weak duplicates in the shop — deck stays at 20 cards.
  • Elite bosses appear every 10 waves from wave 10 onward.

Card Shop

Wave 5 cleared. Buy any card to permanently add it to your deck — but you must remove one of your existing cards in exchange. Higher-wave shops offer rarer cards.
