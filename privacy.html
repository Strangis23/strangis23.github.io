Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Stackwave Defense (“the game”) is available as a browser game and as a desktop build on Steam. This policy describes what data the game stores on your device.

Data stored on your device

High scores, achievements, settings, and lifetime stats are saved locally (localStorage in the browser; Chromium profile storage in the desktop app). This data is not sent to our servers.

You can clear browser data via the high scores page or your browser settings. Steam builds may also sync save data through Steam Cloud when you are signed into Steam — see Valve’s privacy policy for how Steam handles that data.

Advertising (web version only)

When the game is published on a live website, a banner advertisement may appear at the bottom of the screen. Ads are provided by Google AdSense (Google LLC). Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads.

The Steam/desktop build does not include advertising.

Steam

If you play through Steam, your Steam account, achievements, and cloud saves are processed by Valve Corporation under the Steam Subscriber Agreement and Steam Privacy Policy.

Contact

For privacy questions about this game, contact the publisher through the support link on the Steam store page or the website where you play the browser version.
