Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Tetris Tower Defense (“the game”) is a browser game. This policy describes what data the game stores locally and how third-party advertising may collect information when ads are enabled on the hosted version of the site.

Data stored on your device

High scores and game preferences are saved in your browser’s localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers. You can clear it anytime via the “Clear all scores” button on the high scores page or through your browser settings.

Advertising

When the game is published on a live website, a banner advertisement may appear at the bottom of the screen. Ads are provided by Google AdSense (Google LLC). Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted by law and your settings.

Ads do not affect gameplay. There are no rewarded ads and no pay-to-win mechanics tied to advertising.

Learn how Google uses data: Google partner sites policy. You can manage ad personalization at Google Ad Settings.

Children

The game is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through ad services, contact us and we will work to address it.

Contact

For privacy questions about this game, contact the site operator through the contact method listed on the page where you play the game.
